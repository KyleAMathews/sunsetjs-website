SunsetJS

A weekly free + public hangout for JavaScript enthusiasts in Inner Sunset, San Francisco, California. No presentations, RSVPs or startup pitches/overt recruiting allowed. Just show up and talk, learn, and hack!

When & Where

Every Tuesday, 5:30PM - 8:30PM

La Boulange de Sunset
1266 9th Ave San Francisco, CA 94122
1/2 block from the Irving and 9th N-Judah Muni stop
